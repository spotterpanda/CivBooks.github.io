in awe of the architecture. This rail station was massive compared to his home town's! As Nick pulled in he quickly became confused on how to even get outside. Finally, after reading enough signs, he managed to orientate himself West and headed to the shops.

But when Nick got to the coordinates listed for IVC Imports, his heart sank when he realized it had been replaced by a Fossil Corp. store. Nick looked around, trying to see if any other stores were nearby, and locked eyes on one of the biggest: The Monolith Group.
